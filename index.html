About Us

The Psychology Research & Scholars Club (PRSC) at Rowan College at Burlington County is a student-led organization dedicated to exploring psychology through research, discussions, guest speakers, and hands-on projects. Our mission is to foster curiosity, critical thinking, and academic growth while preparing members for transfer to four-year universities.

Whether you're a psychology major or simply interested in understanding human behavior, PRSC offers a supportive environment to learn, lead, and grow academically.
